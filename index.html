What We Do

Our software, PhilanthroMe, will allow for anyone to securely donate to the charity of their choice. This will include an easily accessible sorting and search system for charities and charitable organizations that are all verified to be real and well managed. There will also be detailed descriptions of each charity and links to the charityâ€™s personal sites and information. Within the descriptions there will be various charts describing the general distribution of money that is donated to the charity, allowing the donator to see directly how their money will be used, and for what purposes. This makes it different from sites like GoFundMe where it seems like almost anyone can create their own cause for donation, completely unverified.

Why We Do It

Donating to charity is a process that in the current day is far too complicated relative to the technological equipment our society has to yield. With all the options for charity it is hard to know which charities are real and what charities actually use your donated money for. While it seems like there is a website for everything nowadays, there is no website that can consolidate, verify, and facilitate the donation process for charities. That is where we come in.
